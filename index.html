Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield make a surprise appearance on Jennifer Aniston's AppleTV+ series The Morning Show

Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield made a surprise appearance on AppleTV+'s new release The Morning Show. In an intense scene where Jennifer Aniston's talk show co-host Alex is complaining about cuts to the programme, the pair appeared on the TV in the background. Delightedly showing off the 'blink and you'll miss it' moment on Monday's edition of This Morning, the TV presenting duo were besides themselves with joy.

After the brief scene had finished, Holly added: 'There you go, blink and you miss it!' Some eagle-eyed fans noticed the duos appearance before Monday's show, as they took to Twitter to reveal their discovery.
